Know Your Beauty Type

Learn how to identify your unique skin and hair characteristics for better products, better services, and better results.

Skin Type Analysis

Your skin type determines how products react and perform on your skin. Here's how to identify yours:

  • Normal: Balanced tone, minimal shine or dryness.
  • Dry: Tightness, flaking, dullness after cleansing.
  • Oily: Shine through day, larger pores.
  • Combination: Oily T-zone, dry or normal cheeks.
  • Sensitive: Easily irritated, reactive to temperature/products.

Try this: Wash your face & wait 30 minutes—observe how it feels.

Hair Type Analysis

Your hair’s curl pattern, density, and porosity determine what products work best.

  • Straight (1): Smooth, reflective, may get oily fast.
  • Wavy (2): Loose S-shape, moderate frizz.
  • Curly (3): Defined curls, prone to dryness.
  • Coily (4): Tight curls, delicate and moisture-loving.

Try this: Stretch a wet strand to see curl elasticity.
